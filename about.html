About Fruit.ai

Our Mission

At Fruit.ai, our mission is to make healthy living easy and accessible. We aim to empower users with the tools they need to track and manage their fruit intake, stay informed through translations, and get answers to their health-related questions.

Features

  • Fruit Tracking: Easily log and monitor your fruit consumption with detailed nutritional information.
  • Language Translator: Translate fruit-related content into various languages to support users worldwide.
  • Personalized Recommendations: Get tailored advice and suggestions based on your fruit intake and health goals.
  • FAQ Section: Find answers to common questions and get help with any issues you might encounter.

Meet Our Team

Our team consists of dedicated professionals with a passion for health and technology. From software developers to nutrition experts, we work together to create a product that benefits our users and promotes a healthier lifestyle.
